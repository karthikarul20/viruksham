ELECTRICAL/INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECTS
Through the years we have vast experience in Electrical and Related Infrastructure Projects. Being our part in Renewable Energy Development towards Power Generation, we also have vast experience in Power Transmission and Power Distribution Projects. We had completed Transmission Line works from 11 KV to 33 KV of Single Circuit and Double Circuit Feeder Lines from Power Plants to Substations. Also we have erected 110 / 230 KV Tower Transmission lines for Wind farm developers. We had worked for both LT and HT Transmission Lines in Thermal Power Plant, Wind Power Plant and Solar Power Plants. We also erected DP Transformer structures of 11 /22 / 33 KV line ranging from 40 KVA to 200 KVA in the areas of Tirunelveli and Tuticorin in APDRP Scheme. Moreover we had erected Switchyards for the outgoing feeder lines from the Substation in Wind Industry.

With our skilled manpower and highly equipped system, we also had the experience in Building Electrification from residential, commercial complexes to industries throughout the state.
