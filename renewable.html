RENEWABLE ENERGY DEVELOPMENT
We do consultancy services in promoting Wind Farms and Solar Parks generating green energy to our day to day requirement. All ideas, guidance and support both in technical and commercial will be provided in generating green energy.

WIND POWER DEVELOPMENT

  • All consultancy services from preliminary project report preparation to commissioning of Wind Farm will be provided to our clients through simple charts for their detailed understanding. The services includes the following,

    • Determination of Wind Farm based on requirement
    • Suitability of WTGs
    • Detailed cost working in developing wind farms.
    • Consultancy support from marking to commissioning.
    • Engineering support behind development of wind farms.

  • We deal and consult with all range of Wind Turbines from 250 KW to 2100 KW. A detailed agreement for legalization will be made before every commitments and transactions.

    We also do design of hybrid energy generating systems installing small wind turbines and solar panels in the generation unit for residential, hospital and official building applications.

SOLAR POWER SYSTEMS & DEVELOPMENT

  • We provide with the best consultation and Engineering services of acquiring and managing knowledge with the best solution in setting up a solar power system at your homes, shops, commercial or residential building, telecom towers and agricultural water pumping systems. In producing green energy through solar energy, we basically design the System and execute projects on EPC basis. We analyze the requirement and design accordingly satisfying the domestic needs in compliance to client’s expectation, technical feasibility and commercials adhering to the system.

  • The services include the following,

    • Design against requirement.
    • Detailed cost working in developing design.
    • Supply of Solar Panels, Controllers, Batteries, Hybrid Inverters, and PCU’s.....
    • Engineering support from installation to commissioning.
    • Post Services after Commissioning.

  • We possess a vast experience in designing of Solar Systems and Installation of the same for various loads of residents, schools, colleges, townships, colony and industrial units in ranges of 1 KW to 100 KW. All our earlier performances and experience had made our people more skilled in determining solutions for all trouble shooting factors. A smart and well experienced manpower of us have enabled us to become the best promoter of Solar Power Projects. WE HAVE WORKED IN PROMOTION OF SOLAR PARKS IN MW SERIES.

  • SOLAR SUBMERSIBLE PUMPS

    Similar the way, we do install Solar Submersible Pumps for irrigation, agricultural and industrial needs. We provide Solar Submersible DC pumps with the Solar system for water lifting purposes and also design and install Solar Power system for your existing pumps depending on its load requirement. We have executed pumps from 1 HP to 10 HP in our experience.

  • SOLAR WATER HEATERS

    Our Solar Water Heaters are available in both ETC (Evacuated Tube Collector) and FPC (Flat Plate Collector) models. The justification of models will be made depending on the site factors analyzed thereof. Both the models possess its own significance in technical and commercial aspects. Our Solar Water can be applied for both Domestic and Non-Domestic purposes. For the domestic purposes the Solar Water Heaters are ranged from 100 LPD to 500 LPD whereas in non- domestic purposes Solar Water Heaters are ranged from 500 LPD to 100000 LPD as per requirements.

  • SOLAR STREET LIGHTS

    We do design, manufacture and offer you a comprehensive range of Solar Street Lights for both LED and CFL lights. The design of the system changes depends on the Wattage of lights and type of light. All our Solar Street Lights are with an option of Dusk to Dawn Controllers where an automated lighting is provided with this system. These are widely used in rural electrification, Domestic and Commercial sectors, as well as it is popularly known for its durability and high performance features.

  • SOLAR GARDEN LIGHTS

    We execute the electrification of Garden with Solar Lights depending on the places. All our Solar Garden Lights are vary in their sizes and wattage of Lights. All our Solar Garden lights are provided with Dusk to Dawn feature so that the lights get automatically on at dusk and switches off at dawn with a significant feature of two days autonomy for non sunshine days, (Lumens-100 to 120). Also customized lights also can be supplied upon client’s request.
