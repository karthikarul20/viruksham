CRANES & HEAVY LIFTING MACHINERIES
Our service in crane is more effectively considered by the clients of us through the concept of crane optimization. We do a better service analyzing your crane requirement for the job and business ensuring all the Quality, Safety, Technical and Commercial compliances.

  • CRANE HIRING / RENTAL SERVICES

    With the cranes available in our fleet, we do hire cranes for all natured heavy lifts and have a vast experience in the fields of Wind Turbine Erections, installation of boilers / machinery products , civil construction works, Bridge works, Water Irrigation Projects, Logistics and Cargo handling works on call basis, monthly, quarterly, half-yearly and yearly contracts and provide our best service in support to our client with all type of cranes determined through crane optimization and nature of work.

  • CRANE OPTIMIZATION FOR JOB / PROCUREMENT

    The method of “Optimization” plays a role in selecting the cranes required for the job work and crane procurement. A comparative study on the combinations of two consecutive cranes which suit for the job will be done and the final will be selected calculating commercials of the cranes. A detailed drawing and a calculation can be provided in support to selection and finalization.

    In the task of crane procurement, study on business focused and crane required will be effectively charted ensuring technical and commercial backlogs, complying quality and safety concepts. Also a detailed explanation and justification will be provided for the choice of procuring a new crane or for an option of used cranes calculating the business health, new crane commercials, evaluation of old cranes against depreciation and inspection report.

  • USED CRANES IMPORT / EXPORT

    Our passions on Heavy Lift Cranes have involved us in the transaction of Crane import from foreign countries. A well support in technical aspects will be provided in justification of cranes for your job and business. We have flagged standard business through this concept of crane justification.

    A highly maintained, inspected and evaluated used crane will be recommended for our clients after a satisfaction of us and client against a detailed inspection. Usually our imports of cranes will be from Singapore, Gulf countries and United States of America. The import deal will be with our trust worthy agents and directly with the owners of the cranes in those countries. On request from clients, the inspection, import and export of cranes will be done by us.
